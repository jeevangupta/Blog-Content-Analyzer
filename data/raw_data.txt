<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center" id="its-me-jeevan-gupta">~ It&#8217;s me, Jeevan Gupta. I Help You Achieve Your Goals. Let&#8217;s Work Together!~</h2>
<p>Welcome to Jeevan Gupta&#8217;s World of Tech and Innovation!</p>
<p></p>
<p>I&#8217;m Jeevan Gupta, but you can call me JG or simply G1 for short. I&#8217;m thrilled to have you here and eager to share more about myself with you.</p>
<p></p>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-center"><strong>About Me:</strong></h2>
<p>I come from the beautiful shores of Goa, India, and my early years were spent at St. Andrew&#8217;s Institute in Vasco-da-Gama, a cherished convent school. My educational journey led me to achieve a Bachelor&#8217;s degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from <a href="https://www.klescet.ac.in/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Dr. M.S. Sheshgiri College of Engineering and Technology</a>, which is part of the renowned <a href="https://vtu.ac.in/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Visvesvaraya Technological University</a> in Karnataka, India.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Building upon this strong educational foundation, I pursued a Master&#8217;s degree in Computer Science (MCS) at <a href="https://www.asu.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Arizona State University</a>. All the while, I managed a dynamic role as a Senior Software Engineer at <a href="https://www.bayleafdigital.com/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Bay Leaf Digital (BLD)</a>, a company based in Dallas, Texas, with a focus on Digital Marketing and a vision for SaaS excellence.</p>
<p></p>
<p>With over four years of professional experience in software engineering, I have consistently demonstrated my ability to design, implement, and manage innovative software applications. My true passion in my career lies in tackling complex challenges head-on. I find immense satisfaction in problem-solving, and the end result, no matter how demanding the journey, fuels my motivation and sense of accomplishment.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Throughout my career, I&#8217;ve had the privilege of working on diverse sub-projects that have honed my skills in full-stack development, data pipeline engineering, and process automation. Notably, I&#8217;ve optimized code to reduce technical debt, engineered data pipelines to enhance data availability, and addressed performance bottlenecks in SaaS applications through modular, container-based, and component-based development.</p>
<p></p>
<p>I&#8217;m a dedicated problem solver who relishes the opportunity to dig into technical challenges, identify root causes, and employ cutting-edge technologies to find solutions. My affinity for writing code, particularly in Python, drives me to create, scale, and optimize complex systems. Proficient in translating stakeholder requirements into actionable technical objectives, I excel in project management, product engineering, proof of concepts (POC), technical research, and rapid adaptation to emerging technologies. My unwavering belief in the transformative power of programming fuels my commitment to improving people&#8217;s lives.</p>
<p></p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading has-text-align-left">Entrepreneur Spirit</h3>
<p class="has-text-align-left">To me, entrepreneurship means having an innovative mindset, creativity, and the ability to bring positive change to society. It&#8217;s not just about owning a business; it&#8217;s about having a vision and the determination to make it a reality. Whether you&#8217;re an entrepreneur in business or spirit, it&#8217;s about making a difference.</p>
<div style="height:20px" aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer"></div>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Evolving Face of Entrepreneurship:</h3>
<p>In my view, entrepreneurs are not exclusively business owners. This conventional definition no longer fully encapsulates the spirit of entrepreneurship. Today, anyone who embodies an innovative mindset, creative thinking, and the drive to create positive change can rightfully be called an entrepreneur. It is a state of mind that is characterized by a willingness to explore new possibilities, challenge the established order, and take calculated risks in order to make a difference.</p>
<p></p>
<p>So, whether you&#8217;re steering your own venture or contributing your entrepreneurial spirit within an existing organization, your ability to innovate, inspire change, and leave a positive mark on society is what truly defines you as an entrepreneur.</p>
